Vanguard’s objective

Under conditions of strict confidentiality, assist and facilitate market entry into Mexico by U.S. oil and gas operators. Our diversified menu of assistance is arranged so that the oil and gas expert can remain focused on his economic objective in Mexico. On behalf of the oil and gas operator or service provider, we will manage and navigate the administrative, accounting, legal, tax, labor, security, and even the cultural requirements of doing business in Mexico. We accomplish this with well established, trusted, responsible, transparent, and solution-oriented specialists. These services will be made available to our clients out of our office in Monterrey and or on site at any location in Mexico.

*
The Mexico opportunity

Doing business in Mexico represents both a great opportunity and a challenge for many U.S. firms.To operate successfully in Mexico, it is of critical importance that the U.S. oil and gas firm or specialized service provider operate within the laws, regulations, and guidelines of Mexico.

Our background
-

Our parent firm was created as Vanguard Company, a U.S. dba of a U.S. entrepreneur with dual U.S./Mexican citizenship doing business in Mexico since 1991. During the past 13 years, Vanguard Company has billed out over $520 million USD of product to the United States, Mexico, and Canada on behalf of its U.S. and Mexican partners.

-

To operate in Mexico, this firm encountered and successfully surmounted a significant learning curve on “How to do business in Mexico”. This new, specialized affiliated firm, Vanguard & Company S.A. de C.V. was created with the purpose of packaging the parent company’s learning process, in collaboration with selected, highly experienced and reputable specialists, dedicated to providing support to oil and gas firms and service providers interested in entry into Mexico’s oil and gas sector, to achieve that entry efficiently and expeditiously, with complete confidentiality.

-

This was a need created by recent changes to Mexico’s Constitution relative to participation by extra national entities in drilling and production of oil and natural gas resources, and related services, previously prohibited from participating under the Constitution. The new environment in the energy sector is relatively complicated by legal, regulatory and other requirements that must be complied with by non-Mexican firms seeking to enter the market. Vanguard & Company S.A. de C.V. was formed as a corporate composite of reputable professionals from diverse backgrounds with decades of experience and a broad range of expertise who would be able to meet and assist, in part or in whole, with the entry process and reliable maintenance of every day operations for a U.S. enterprise in Mexico.

About Us

·
*

Vanguard provides high value market and business development and sales outsourcing solutions to companies in the food industry in North America. Clients receive value through brand building and optimum sales performance.

*

Vanguard is not a broker and is not an agent. Vanguard works as part of client’s company as their in-house market and sales organization. Vanguard works with a policy of complete transparency. This way, our clients have full knowledge of who they are working with at all times.

*

As a seamless extension to a client’s operations, Vanguard provides an expert marketing and sales team which brings ease of management, effective market development and improved sales channels.

Through its sales team headquartered in Monterrey, Mexico, Vanguard assumes the identity of its supply partners by managing all aspects of market development, branding, sales, invoicing and collections. We connect our growers with US and Canadian consumers while safeguarding all shipments through contracts with US-based agricultural laboratories and cold storage facilities.

Vanguard’s Monterrey-based sales team has earned its reputation for transparency and integrity in its dealings with growers, users and regulators. We are in an ideal position to meet the upsurge in consumer demand for safe produce of consistent quality, a demand reinforced by media, nutritionist, food marketers, public health officials and policy makers.

Vanguard has deliberately positioned itself to assure additional deliveries of wholesome produce and other products to its expanding list of clients in North America.
